About Me

Hello, I am Himanshu, an electronics engineer and hobbyist game designer with over 12 years of game design development experience. My expertise spans the design, development, and publishing of 2D, 3D, and VR & AR games. Proficient with Unity Ads, Admob, and skilled in research, analysis, game documentation, and ensuring seamless gameplay using MDA framework and playtesting. I hold several game design certifications from HP. I have experience as a Steam game modder, Roblox content creator (since 2007), and I livestream on Twitch, YouTube, and Kick. A passionate retro gamer, retro game and retro computer collector. I’m eager to network, collaborate, and contribute to the gaming industry. I have established a young startup in India and I am legally authorized to work in India, USA and Canada without a visa or sponsorship.
